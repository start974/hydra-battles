Definition hyd (a b c : nat) :=

The natural number N is expressed in terms of the (iterate doubleS) function. The rest of this file is dedicated to get an intuition of how it is huge . Our idea is to get a minoration by exponentials of base 2

Definition exp2 n := iterate (fun n ‚áí 2 √ó n) n 1.

Lemma exp2S : ‚àÄ n, exp2 (S n) = 2 √ó exp2 n.

Lemma minoration_0 : ‚àÄ n, 2 √ó n ‚â§ doubleS n.

Lemma minoration_1 : ‚àÄ n x, exp2 n √ó x ‚â§ iterate doubleS n x.

Lemma minoration_2 : exp2 95 √ó 95 ‚â§ M.

Lemma minoration_3 : exp2 (S M) √ó S M ‚â§ N.

Lemma exp2_mono1 : ‚àÄ n p, n ‚â§ p ‚Üí exp2 n ‚â§ exp2 p .

Lemma minoration : exp2 (exp2 95 √ó 95) ‚â§ N.

Hence, the length of the battle is greater or equal than exp2 (exp2 95 * 95), a number whose representation in base 10 has at least 10 ^ 30 digits !
